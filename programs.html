Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4 years old, and focuses on play-based learning and socialization.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves grades K-5, and emphasizes hands-on learning, critical thinking, and creativity.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School program serves grades 6-12, and offers a rigorous and personalized education that prepares students for success in college and beyond.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program provides students with hands-on experience in science, technology, engineering, and math, and prepares them for careers in these fields.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program offers students the opportunity to explore their creativity through music, drama, visual arts, and other disciplines.

🏊‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program provides students with the opportunity to develop their physical skills and teamwork abilities, and compete with other schools in the area.
