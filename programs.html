Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-5, focusing on play-based learning, social-emotional development, and building a strong foundation for future academic success.

📚

Elementary

In our elementary program, students engage in hands-on learning experiences that foster curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. We emphasize the development of essential skills in reading, writing, and mathematics.

💡

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs offer a rigorous and engaging curriculum that prepares students for success in college and beyond. We emphasize the development of critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills.

🔬

STEM

The Paideia School's STEM program offers a comprehensive and hands-on approach to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics education. We emphasize inquiry-based learning, experimentation, and critical thinking.

🎭

Arts

Our arts program offers a wide range of creative experiences in music, drama, visual arts, and dance. We believe that the arts are essential to a well-rounded education and offer students a unique opportunity to express themselves and explore their creativity.

🏆

Athletics

The Paideia School's athletic program offers a range of competitive and recreational sports experiences for students of all skill levels. We emphasize the development of teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness.
