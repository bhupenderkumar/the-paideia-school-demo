At The Paideia School, we strive to create a supportive and challenging environment that allows every student to flourish. As Head of School, I am proud to lead a community that values curiosity, creativity, and kindness. Our students, faculty, and staff work together to make a difference in the lives of others.

Our Mission & History

The Paideia School was founded in 1970 as a progressive alternative to traditional Atlanta schools. Our history is rooted in a commitment to academic excellence and social responsibility.

Our teaching approach is centered around student-centered learning, project-based education, and a focus on the arts and humanities. Our faculty are passionate about helping students develop a love of learning that will last a lifetime.

From athletics to community service, The Paideia School offers a wide range of extracurricular activities that help students develop important life skills, build relationships, and cultivate a sense of purpose and belonging.
